The Grand Egyptian Museum (GEM) is under construction near the Pyramids of Giza. Designed by Heneghan Peng Architects, it will be the world's largest archaeological museum complex. The opening was delayed due to environmental, financial and political reasons, but is now expected for mid-2024. The GEM will house over 100,000 artifacts, including King Tut's entire treasure collection.
